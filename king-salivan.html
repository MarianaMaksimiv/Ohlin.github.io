212 WEST 18TH STREET
NEW YORK, NY 10011


A building without peer, Walker Tower has redefined the market and raised the bar for luxury living downtown. Constructed in 1929 by the legendary architect Ralph Thomas Walker, hailed in The New York Times as the “architect of the century”, Walker Tower has been transformed into 50 luxury condominiums. The elaborate brick façade has been painstakingly restored, as has the Art Deco ornamentation that Walker made famous through architectural masterpieces including One Wall Street and the Barclay-Vesey Building. Built before neighborhood height limits were enacted, Walker Tower rises high above its surrounding, enjoying stunning and protected 360-degree Manhattan views. Sweeping interior spaces, soaring ceilings reaching nearly 14 feet, large new tilt-and-turn windows, grand private terraces, custom-designed finishes and a comprehensive amenities package make Walker Tower a pre-war icon and modern-day marvel.
